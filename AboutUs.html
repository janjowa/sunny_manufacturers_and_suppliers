About Us


Our company is located in Sialkot Pakistan which is very well known city around the world for its sports goods & Apparel production.Sialkot is producing 50% of the World Soccer Balls and this capacity is being increased each year. Apart from the soccers being produced in factories, 30% of the soccers are Manufacturered in cottage industries by village women. We specialize in producing sublimated & non sublimated apparels. we continually innovate functional fabrics and develop new construction techniques in house to produce market leading products.

SMS - Sunny Manufacturers and Suppliers is one of the Pakistan’s leading Medical instruments Supplier, Sports Items Manufacturer & Garments manufacturer especially custom made uniforms,Tracksuits, Cycling clothing and Compression wears, at the forefront of concept customized design, prototype innovation and full scale production; producing and distributing Products of the highest quality to small and medium sized Brands.
We have been the manufacturing partners for sportswear brands from USA, EUROPE AND GCC. We are serving our customers with a complete sportswear manufacturing solution from design to delivery and also custom full package production and that too, at FACTORY DIRECT PRICES.
We at SMS - Sunny Manufacturers and Suppliers have a state-of-the-art manufacturing setup; it is equipped with most modern and advanced technologies. This combination with our highly experienced management has enabled the company to become one of the best sportswear manufacturer in the region. We have strategically placed manufacturing facilities, which gives us the capability to be able to produce items at both low quantities and bulk alike. As a result of constant innovation, SMS is proud to offer industry leading minimum order quantities for the production of custom goods.

Our service extended from producing custom-made cricket, soccer, hockey and other sports clothing for amateur teams to developing a complete range of merchandise for professional organizations completed with embroidered logo and printed name & numbers etc.
